SETU Home Page

South East Technological University is the first technological university in south east Ireland.

This gives us an exciting platform to establish our community as a centre for innovation, opportunity, and growth.

SETU offers tuition and research programs in various areas up to PhD level and has a total of nine campuses spread across Waterford, Kilkenny, Carlow, and Wexford.
